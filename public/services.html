Web Integration

I will integrate your design in HTML CSS & JS code

A web integration is a phase that allows the final mock-up of a website or web application to be made into a web page.

If you are a designer you may need to convert your design into actual code. As a frontend developer I will create structured and efficient HTML, CSS and JS code to transform your work into a HTML website.

If necessary I can also convert your design into a wordpress website.

Process

My web integration will follow the 6 steps below:

  • 1. Separation of contents, work on design and layout of the graphic elements

  • 2. Integration of pages in HTML5 & CSS3 according to web standards

  • 3. Addition of Javascript code for interactivity (if applicable)

  • 4. Optimization of accessibility

  • 5. Optimization of all elements related to referencing

  • 6. Check compatibility with browsers and devices

How does it work?

In order for me to code your design exactly as you conceived it, I will need a detailed PSD, Figma or Sketch file of both desktop and mobile design, includieng the required assets such as fonts, images, colors, etc..

HTML & CSS are the markup languages I will use to organize and style the layout of your design. If necessary, I will use JavaScript to add specific features and interactivity.

Once I have finished the web integration I will send you a zip file with the required HTML, CSS and JS files.

FAQ

It is common that people ask me some of these questions before starting a project with me:

What kind of projects do you build?

For website creation I mainly focus on professional business & e-commerce websites but I can also create blog or portfolio websites for example.

For web applications and interfaces I focus on frontend development so it will mainly depend if your app requires a lot of backend or not.

In any case, I would be happy to discuss any project you may have.

Do you build landing page and multi-pages website?

Yes, I will build the number of pages you need for your website. I can build both landing page and multi-pages website. As they don’t have the same purpose, I use different methods, let’s discuss it to see which format fits the most with your business.

How long will it take to build my website/web app?

It will mainly depend on the size and complexity of your site.

Other factors like going for a template or having your design ready will reduce the design process time.

It can go from 1/2 a day for a shrot integration, 2 days for a landing page to several days/weeks for a more complex website and web application that will require experimental & research.

How much will it cost me?

It mainly depends on the size and complexity of your project.

I charge my clients 300€ per day worked (or 50€ per hour if less than 1 day).

For example, for an optimized and converting landing page that will take me 2 days to complete it will cost you 600€. A short HTML integration in a website that require ½ day will only cost you 150€. A full e-commerce website may require at least 4-5 days of work. For app development it depends on the complexity and the necessary time of research it requires.

Should I expect other costs?

If it is a new website, you will have to buy a domain name and a host plan. It should cost you around 50$ per year.

You don't have to worry about the set up. I will take care of it.

Will you use a Content Management System such as Wordpress?

Yes I can, but it depends on your needs and/or the tools you already use (in case of website update).

If you already have a Wordpress website (or any CMS) I can integrate new content or make any modification on the design and layout, using Wordpress tools or customized code.

If you don’t have a website yet and you know you will need to update the content and make minor design modifications by yourself I would recommend a Wordpress website. If you prefer a fully customizable and fast loading website and you don’t intend to often change the content I would recommend an HTML website.

For e-commerce website I will use Wordpress associated with WooCommerce.

Will you use a template and customize it or start from scratch with a unique design?

It depends on your needs.

If you are limited on time and budget and just need a simple website to introduce your business it may be a good idea to use a pre-made template website.

On the other hand, if you want a website that really fits your business goals and brand identity, I would recommend a fully customized website with a unique design.

Will you make the design of the website user friendly?

Yes, that is my main focus when developing any web project.

As the end goal of any website is to be used by as many visitors and customers as possible I intend to make it enjoyable to use. I use my experience in marketing to optimize the accessibility of my projects.

What content will you include in my website?

I will include the content you consider important for your users.

My job is to make sure I showcase and highlight your content in the most efficient way to achieve the goals the goals we defined.

I am not a content writer though. I believe you know your business and your clients better than anyone so you will be the most qualified person to write the content of your website.

Will I be able to sell my products online?

Yes! If you want to sell your product or services on your website I will create an e-commerce structure for you.

You will be able to manage your online store, monitor the sales, stocks, deliveries.

I can also enable online payment so that you customers pay on the website and you directly receive the money on your bank account.

Will it work on both desktop and mobile?

Yes, I optimize all my sites and applications to be fully usable on all devices, including mobile phone, tablet, laptop and larger screen.

Where will you publish my website?

I will publish and host your website on the hosting platform that is most suitable for your website.

For Wordpress websites I usually go for OVH or Namecheap as they offer affordable and secure hosting plans as well as domains name purchase.

For static HTML website and frontend web applications I mainly use platforms like Vercel and Netifly.

I already have a domain name and & hosting, can you still work on my website?

Yes, I can work with any hosting platform.

My abilities to work on your website will only depend on the tools and languages used to build your websites. Let’s discuss it to see if I can assist.

How can I be sure it will have a good ranking on Search Engines such as Google?

I will do all the necessary work to make sure it is compliant with all the requirements from Search Engines.

However, please understand that Search engines ranking depend on many factors and actual results may take time to happen after optimizations have been made (few weeks to few months).

How can I make sure it will lead to conversions? And how can I measure these conversions?

As your goals will be defined before the development of your website, I will optimize the design and development of the website and include clear call to actions to make sure it maximizes the number of conversions.

To measure these conversions I will set up a google analytics account that will be linked to your website. You will be able to monitor metrics related to your traffic, demographics, conversions on a daily, weekly and monthly basis for example.

It can be really soon.

For website or web app development I will need to ask you a few questions about your project and business before I can get started.
