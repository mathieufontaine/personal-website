I take care of the purchase of the domain name, the publication as well as the hosting of your site on servers, such as OVH, namecheap, netifly..

You need a website to: highlight your services? sell your products, share information?

For the creation of your site I offer 3 types of packages according to your needs:

Static Website is made for people who need a very simple website to showcase their services. A landing page website is often the best choice if you don't sell anything online and you only want your clients to know about your business so that they can come to your store or contact you.

E-commerce Website is made for people who need a more interactive and customizable website to showcase and/or sell their services or products. You sell offline but your customers mainly find you online. You want to sell your services online or you have a limited number of products you want to sell online. In that case a 3 to 6 pages Website is recommended as you want more advanced functionalities to your website (such as a reservations calendar, online payment, etc..). You will also want to split your content and highlight specific information on each page.

Full E-commerce Website is made for people who mostly sell their products or services online and need a complete and very customizable e-commerce website. The whole conversion tunnel happens online, from brand recognition to actual purshase. You have an important number of products to sell and need a unique page per article. You may also need a Blog section etc.. In that case your website will include more than 10 pages with custom functionalities according to your specific needs.
