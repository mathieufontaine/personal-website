About Me

"I am Mathieu and I love building nice websites and web/mobile appications"

Web Developper

I am a freelance web developper. I learned fullstack development at Le Wagon in 2019 (Ruby/JS/React..). Since the end of the Bootcamp I practiced a lot by doing various personnal projects. I also acquired new skills on different technologies (back and front), learning by myself.

Passionate about Frontend

What I enjoy the most about coding is the ability to instantly see the results of your work. Thus, I naturally decided to specialize in frontend development. I focus on writing accessible HTML, using modern CSS practices and writing clean Javascript code.

Working Remotely

I am currently based in Paris but I exclusively work remotely. I am much more productive when I work in my own environment. When I am not coding you will find me outdoors. I love exporing new places by foot or with my bike.
