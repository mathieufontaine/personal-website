My Path

I have learned and done many things in the past 10 years. I have also lived in different places.

The next pages show, through different cities, the main professional path I followed (studies & jobs) until 2020.

Toulouse

2011-2016

Toulouse School of Management

Master in Marketing of New Technologies

I am from Paris but at 18 I decided to go to the South of France, in Toulouse, to study Economics & Marketing.

After 2 years at the Toulouse School of Economics and 3 years at the Toulouse School of Management I succesfully got a Bachelor and a Master degree in Marketing.

Singapore

2015-2016

Top Serve

Web Project Manager

During my gap year in 2015/2016 I went to Singapore and worked for a startup called TOPSERVE for 9 months. There, I was in charge of the digital development of the company.

I completely rebuilt their website and significantly improved their SEO, SEA, CRM and Social Media performance.

Bangkok

2017

Assumption University of Thailand

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

After a very rewarding experience in Singapore I intended to extend my business knowledges abroad and I spent a year in Bangkok, at the Assumption University of Thailand.

There, I successfully passed my Master of Business Administration (MBA).

Paris

2018-2019

Club Med

Online Media Project Manager

In 2018 I decided to come back to my hometown. I worked at the Club Med's headquarters as online media project manager for 18 months.

I was in charge of some of the biggest European SEA accounts of the company, including UK, DE, IT. After succesfully contributed to improve the online media performance of the company I decided to leave for a new challenge.

Lisbon

2019

Le Wagon

Fullstack Web Development Bootcamp

After quitting my position at Club Med I went straight to Lisbon to learn fullstack web development.

There, I joined Le wagon bootcamp in September 2019 for a 3 months intensive program

At Le Wagon I gained tremendous knowledges and skills about both backend (Ruby/SQL) and frontend (HTML/CSS/Javacript/React) development.
